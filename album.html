01 · Strategic Framing

How We Position This Album

For By Grace is not a collection of all-new songs. It is a curated album moment that anchors existing releases alongside new material — strengthening the Pneuma catalogue rather than starting from zero.

We Will
  • ✓Frame For By Grace as a curated album bringing together the best of Pneuma's worship output
  • ✓Keep Gathering of Believers, Responsible Father, and Inhabit as released anchors that give the album instant credibility
  • ✓Use For By Grace as the key promotional single in the final week before release
  • ✓Drop the full album on 29 August with a coordinated launch campaign
  • ✓Extend album life post-release by dropping videos for Worthy, Anchor and other tracks over the following months
  • ✓Build 6+ months of content from a single album cycle
We Will Not
  • ✗Release every remaining unreleased song as a single before the album — exhausting all assets before launch
  • ✗Position the album as entirely new music when anchor songs are already streaming
  • ✗Front-load all video content before release day — spread it across 6 months post-launch
  • ✗Confirm a release date before Worthy and Anchor audio are complete and all assets are ready
  • ✗Launch without a date-by-date execution plan owned by the team

02b · Release Architecture

Three-Tier Release Strategy

Audio first. Lyrics same day. Official videos over six months. This architecture maximises YouTube watch hours, sustains momentum, and gives every song a visual presence from day one.

Tier 2 · Same Day or Within 48 Hours
Lyric Album on YouTube

  • →Upload lyric videos or visualizers for every track — especially songs without finished videos
  • →Create a full By Grace Lyric Album playlist on YouTube — pin everywhere

03 · 6-Week Hype Runway

Tease → Announce → Single → Story → Lyric Album → Drop

Album drops Friday 29 August. The runway starts mid-July — six weeks of building anticipation, not a two-week announcement. Each week has a distinct purpose and escalating energy.

W1
Week 1
~14–18 Jul
Album Tease
  • "Something is coming…" teaser across IG and TikTok — no reveal yet
  • Start using album language quietly: By Grace, grace, worship, testimony
  • Behind-the-scenes clips from recording sessions — faces, rooms, moments
  • Soft announce the theme: grace, encounter, what this album cost
W2
Week 2
~21–25 Jul
Official Announcement
  • Reveal album title — For By Grace — and cover art
  • Announce release date: Friday 29 August 2026
  • Drop tracklist across all platforms
  • Launch pre-save on Spotify and Apple Music immediately
  • Begin WhatsApp, church, and Zoe community push — Zainab + campus media heads activated
W3
Week 3
~28 Jul–1 Aug
Single Push — For By Grace
  • Release For By Grace as the album focus single
  • Drop lyric video or visualizer same day
  • Flood short-form with audio clips — Reels, TikToks, Stories
  • Campus leaders share simultaneously via coordinated WhatsApp blast
  • Push pre-save alongside the single — direct link in every post
