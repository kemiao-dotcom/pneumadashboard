01 · Identity

Who We Are

A worship ministry that integrates scripture, melody, and encounter — production always in service of the Spirit, never performance.

"Worship is beyond songs and melodies. We see what we sing."

Pneuma Worship Collective is the worship music expression of Zoe Household Global, led by Pastor Dolapo Lawal. Based in Lagos (Yaba and Ikeja branches), Atlanta, and Houston, with active presence in Abeokuta and London.

We produce live worship recordings and original contemporary Christian music that integrates scripture and theology with congregational worship. Every song is a theological statement. Every production decision serves encounter.

We are not just a worship team. We are building the creative nerve centre for Nigerian and African worship — songs that congregations adopt, content that reaches diaspora believers, and events that create culture.

  • 2020Jubilee Choir begins writing and recording original songs intentionally. Vision shifts from congregational service to original worship output.
  • 2024Rebranded as Pneuma Worship Collective. Debut live album Pneuma (Live) released. Singles Amen Hallelujah and Abba's Beloved drop.
  • 2025Inhabit released. Gbagada studio conversion begins. Strategy team formalises with cross-continent membership (Jan 2026).
  • 2026Gathering of Believers + Responsible Father video released. First TOWDAH sells out at 100. Opemipo + Pipe join team. Pleroo and Amen Hallelujah videos ready. Album drops 10 July 2026.

Revenue Model — Dual Scenario

Nigeria-Heavy: Grows domestic streams and watch hours faster — easier to scale community, but streaming revenue per play is a fraction of US/UK rates. Channel location and ad types also differ.

Diaspora-Heavy: Target UK, US, Canada via ad region selection + Zoe ATL collaborations. Higher revenue per stream. Slower community build but the long-term financial flywheel.

Strategic insight (from team discussion Feb 2026): Viewership location matters more than channel location for monetisation. Priority: grow viewership in high-value markets via targeted ads and diaspora network activation.
